Understanding Your Invoice

Invoice Components
  • Invoice Number: Unique identifier for your invoice
  • Issue Date: When the invoice was created
  • Due Date: Payment deadline
  • Participant Count: Number of registered attendees
  • Status: Current state of the invoice
Pricing Structure
  • 1-3 Participants: KES 15,000 per person
  • 4 Participants: KES 45,000 total
  • 5+ Participants: KES 11,000 per person
