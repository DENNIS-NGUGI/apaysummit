Step-by-Step Registration Process

1

Register Your Participants

Add all individuals who will be attending the Apay Summit.

  • Click on "Register Participants" from your dashboard
  • Fill in participant details (name, email, phone)
  • You can add multiple participants at once
  • Participants will be automatically added to your current invoice
Add Participants
2

Navigate to Invoices

View your generated invoice with all registered participants.

  • Go to "My Invoices" from the main menu or dashboard
  • View your invoice details and total amount
  • Check the number of participants and pricing breakdown
  • Download the PDF invoice for your records
View Invoices
3

Make Payment

Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction.

  • Bank Transfer: Use the provided bank details
  • Mobile Money: Use PayBill number 522522
  • Always include your invoice number as reference
  • Keep your payment confirmation/receipt
Payment Methods Available
4

Upload Proof of Payment

Submit evidence of your payment for verification.

  • Go to your invoice details page
  • Click "Upload Proof" button
  • Select your payment proof file (PDF, JPG, PNG)
  • Add payment method and reference notes
  • Submit for admin verification
Upload Proof
